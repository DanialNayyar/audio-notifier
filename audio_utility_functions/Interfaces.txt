events.py:
  Defines allowed events
  - Success
  - Error
  - High Score
  - Low Score
  - Training Complete





sound_config.py
- Defines SoundConfig
- Validates frequency, duration, waveform, volume

  Validation rules:
  - frequency > 0
  - duration > 0
  - waveform = one of allowed waveforms
  - 0 < volume <=1



event_selector.py
 - choose_score_event (score_achieved, threshold)


sound_presets.py
  - get_sound_config(event)



waveform_generator.py:
  -generate_waveform(sound_config, sample_rate)

  - Options:
      - Sine
      - Square
      - Triangle
      - Sawtooth
      - Custom:


sound_player.py:
- play_sound()
